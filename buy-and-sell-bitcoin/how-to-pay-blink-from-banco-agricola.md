# How to pay Blink from Banco Agricola?

1. Open the Banco Agricola app and tap `Transferencias Transfer365`\
    ![](../.gitbook/assets/banco_agricola_app01.png)
2. Fill the [Blink account details](../buy-and-sell-bitcoin/what-are-the-details-of-blinks-bank-account.md) and the `Unique Code` of the order\
    ![](../.gitbook/assets/banco_agricola_app02.png)\
    ![](../.gitbook/assets/banco_agricola_app03.png)
3. Tap `TRANSFERIR` to send the transfer.
