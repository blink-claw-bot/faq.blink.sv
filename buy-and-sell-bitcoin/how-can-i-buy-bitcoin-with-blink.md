# How can I buy bitcoin with Blink?

1. Open the Blink app and tap the price graph icon on the top left
2. Tap  `Buy and sell bitcoin` on the bottom of the screen
3. Choose the country where your bank account is
4. Tap the  `Buy Bitcoin` button
5. Enter the amount to buy in USD
6. Confirm your email to be used to send your order details (this is auto-filled if you have an email set for the account)&#x20;
7. Tap `Create order`
8. The bank transfer details will be presented in this screen&#x20;
9. The order will processed automatically processed once Blink receives the money, but can send an email to [support@blink.sv](mailto:support@blink.sv) when you have initiated the transfer
