# How can I sell bitcoin with Blink?

1. Open the Blink app and tap the price graph icon on the top left

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot01.jpg" width="300">

2. Tap  `Buy and sell bitcoin` on the bottom of the screen

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot02.jpg" width="300">

3. Choose the country where your bank account is and tap the `Sell Bitcoin` button

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot03.jpg" width="300">

5. Enter the amount to sell in USD, fill your bank details where you will receive the transfer and tap `Next`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot07.jpg" width="300">

6. Fill in your details matching the details on your bank account, enter an email where you can be contacted and tap `Next`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot08.jpg" width="300">

8. Confirm the order details containing your name and bank account number (redacted here) and tap `Confirm`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot09.jpg" width="300">

9.  Choose if you would like to pay from your BTC or USD (Stablesats) balance and tap `Next` to pay

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot10.jpg" width="300">

10. The order will be processed automatically, but it can take between 1 hour to 1 day for the money to reach your bank account. Please monitor your email address for updates and contact [support@blink.sv](mailto:support@blink.sv) if you have any concerns.
