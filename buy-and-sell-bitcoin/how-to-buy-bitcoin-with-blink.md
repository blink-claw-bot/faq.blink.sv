# How can I buy bitcoin with Blink?

1. Open the Blink app and tap the price graph icon on the top left

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot01.jpg" width="300">

2. Tap  `Buy and sell bitcoin` on the bottom of the screen

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot02.jpg" width="300">

3. Choose the country where your bank account is and tap the `Buy Bitcoin` button

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot03.jpg" width="300">

4. Enter the amount to buy in USD

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot04.jpg" width="300">

5. Confirm your email to be used to send your order details to (this is auto-filled if you have an email set for the account) and tap `Create order`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot05.jpg" width="300">

6.  The bank transfer details will be presented on this screen (always check the exact details - redacted here)

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot06.jpg" width="300">

7. The payment initiated from your bank account may take between 1 hour and 1 day to be processed and arrive on the bank account of Blink. Once the order is complete you will receive a notification and the bitcoin will become available in your Blink wallet. Please monitor your email address for updates and contact [support@blink.sv](mailto:support@blink.sv) if you have any concerns.
