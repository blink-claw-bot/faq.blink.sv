# How to pay Blink from Banco Cuscatlan?

1. Open the Banco Cuscatlan app and tap `Transfer365`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/banco_cuscatlan_app01.png" width="300">

2. Tap `Otro` and fill in the [Blink account details](../buy-and-sell-bitcoin/what-are-the-details-of-blinks-bank-account.md) and the `Unique Code` of the order

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/banco_cuscatlan_app02.png" width="300">

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/banco_cuscatlan_app03.png" width="300">

3. Tap `Continuar` to send the transfer.
