# Is it possible to change the phone number associated with a username after creating a Blink account?

Yes, it is possible to change the phone number associated with your username after creating a Blink account. Follow these steps to update your phone number:

1. Go to the "Settings" section in your Blink app.
1. Navigate to the "Account" settings
1. Ensure that you have associated an email address with your account for account recovery purposes.
1. Delete the current phone number associated with your account.
1. Add the new phone number that you wish to associate with your Blink account.

By following these steps, you can update your phone number within the Blink app. This allows you to maintain accurate and up-to-date contact information associated with your account.
