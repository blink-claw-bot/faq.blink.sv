# How do I create an account with Blink?

Download the Blink application from your phone's app store or [use the APK on Android](/creating-a-blink-account/how-to-install-the-apk-from-github.md).

Launch the app — a Trial account is automatically assigned. No further steps required to start using Blink.

## Save your Blink ID

Go to Settings > Your account. Copy your alphanumeric Blink ID and save it somewhere safe. If you ever lose access and have not set a username, this is the only way to locate your account.

## Set your username (optional)

The username is your internal Blink identifier and also serves as your [Lightning address](/blink-payment-methods/README.md).

1. Select `Settings` in the upper right corner of the Home Screen
2. Select `Your Blink address` and click `Set your Blink Address`
3. Choose a username for your account. Your username should not have any special characters or spaces. If your desired username is not available, you can add numbers or more letters to check for availability.
4. Click on `Set Your Blink Address` to finalize.

You are now set up to send and receive bitcoin over the Lightning Network, LNURL, and to other Blink users (Intraledger). [Daily limits apply.](/using-blink/what-are-blinks-account-limits.md)

Need on-chain payments, higher limits, or API access? Add a phone number to upgrade to Level 1.

## Add a phone number

1. Select the flag based on the area code of your phone number
2. Enter your phone number and swipe right to complete the puzzle
3. Enter the code received by SMS

## Add an email address (optional)

Adding an email address lets you log in without relying on your phone number.

1. Select the menu on the top right and tap `Account`
2. Select `Email (for login)`
3. Enter an email address and complete the verification to enable login with email
