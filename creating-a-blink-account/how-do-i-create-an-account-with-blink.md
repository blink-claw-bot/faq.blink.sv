# How do I create an account with Blink?

Creating an Blink account is a simple process that involves the following steps:

1. Download the Blink application from your phone's app store.&#x20;
2. Select _Settings_  in the upper right corner of the Home Screen
3. Select the flag based on the area code of your phone number
4. Enter your phone number and swipe left to complete the puzzles.
5. Enter the code received by SMS&#x20;
6. Select _**Settings**_&#x20;
7. Select **Your Blink address** and click in _**Set your Blink Address**_
8. Choose a username for your account. Your username should not have any special characters or spaces. If your desired username is not available, you can add numbers or more letters to check for availability.
9. Click in _**Set Your Blink Address**_ to finalize the creation of your account
