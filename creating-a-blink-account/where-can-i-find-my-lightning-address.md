# Where can I find my Lightning address?

You can find your Lightning address within the Blink app. Here's how:

1. Open the Blink app on your mobile device.
2. Navigate to the "Settings" section.
3. Select "Your Blink Address."
4. Your username, which also serves as your internal Blink identifier and Lightning address, will be displayed on the screen.

Additionally, you can access your Lightning address by following these steps:

1. Open the Blink app on your mobile device.
2. Navigate to the "Receive" section.
3. Select the Lightning Network tab.
4. Your Lightning address will be displayed on the screen. It typically starts with "ln" followed by a string of characters.
