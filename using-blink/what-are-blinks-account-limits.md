# What are Blink's account limits?

Blink's accounts limits levels are the following:&#x20;

| <p><br></p>                 | **Level 0**     | **Level 1**      | **Level 2**      |
| --------------------------- | --------------- | ---------------- | ---------------- |
| **Receive Transactions**    | Unlimited       | Unlimited        | Unlimited        |
| **Withdraw Transactions**   | $125 per day    | $999 per day     | $50,000 per day  |
| **Send to Blink User**      | $125 per day    | $999 per day     | $50,000 per day  |
| **Stablesats Conversion**   | $2,000 per day  | $50,000 per day  | $500,000 per day |
