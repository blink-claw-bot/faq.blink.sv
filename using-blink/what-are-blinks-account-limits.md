# What are Blink's account limits?

Blink's accounts limits levels are the following:&#x20;

| <p><br></p>               | **Level 0**     | **Level 1**      | **Level 2**      |
| ------------------------- | --------------- | ---------------- | ---------------- |
| **Receive Transactions**  | Unlimited       | Unlimited        | Unlimited        |
| **Withdraw Transactions** | $125 per day    | $2,000 per day   | $50,000 per day  |
| **Send to Blink User**    | $125 per day    | $4,000 per day   | $50,000 per day  |
| **Stablesats Transfer**   | $2,000 per day  | $10,000 per day  | $100,000 per day |
