# What will I find on the main screen of my Blink account?

1. **Balance Display:** Your current balance, shown in both BTC (Bitcoin) and Stablesats (USD-equivalent balance).
2. **Action Buttons:** Convenient buttons for various actions, allowing you to convert between currencies, receive Bitcoin, send payments, or scan QR codes.
3. **Transaction History:** A list of your recent transactions, providing a record of your incoming and outgoing payments.
