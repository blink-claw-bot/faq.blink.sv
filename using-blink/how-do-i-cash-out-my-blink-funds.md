# How do I cash out my Blink funds?

To withdraw your Blink funds, you can use one of the following methods:

1. Sell your Bitcoin from peer to peer to exchange it for Fiat currency.
2. Find a Bitcoin ATM near you using a website like [Coin Atm Radar](https://coinatmradar.com/).
3. Sell your Bitcoin through a centralized exchange like Bitfinex, Kraken, or others that support Blink.&#x20;

Please note that fees may apply and the availability of these methods may vary. It's always recommended to research and compare the fees and rates of different providers before choosing a method to cash out your funds
