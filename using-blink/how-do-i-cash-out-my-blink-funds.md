# How do I cash out my Blink funds?

To withdraw your Blink funds, you can use the following methods:

1. For users in El Salvador who have [upgraded their account to level 2](https://faq.blink.sv/using-blink/how-can-i-change-my-transaction-limits-on-my-blink-account) selling and buying bitcoin is available with Blink:
    - In the Blink app:
      - Tap the bitcoin price graph icon on the upper left corner of the Blink app
      - Tap the "Buy or sell bitcoin" button on the bottom of the screen
    - Alternatively can use the website: [fiat.blink.sv](https://fiat.blink.sv)
2. Sell bitcoin peer to peer to exchange it for a fiat currency.
3. Find a Bitcoin ATM near you using a website like [Coin Atm Radar](https://coinatmradar.com/).
4. Sell your Bitcoin through a centralized exchange like Bitfinex, Kraken, or others that support Blink.&#x20;

Please note that fees may apply dependent on the exchange method you use and the availability of these methods may vary. It's always recommended to research and compare the fees and rates of different providers before choosing a method to cash out your funds.
