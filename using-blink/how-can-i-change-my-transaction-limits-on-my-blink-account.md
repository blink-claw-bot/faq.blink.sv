# How can I change my transaction limits on my Blink account?

If you need to change or increase the transaction limits on your Blink account, please contact our support team through your preferred channel. Our team will guide you through the process and help you with the necessary steps to request a change in your account limits.&#x20;

You can contact us through email at [support@blink.sv](mailto:support@bbw.sv), via WhatsApp at +503 6983-5115.

We are always ready to assist you and answer any questions you may have.
