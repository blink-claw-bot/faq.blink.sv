# How does the earn section work?

The Earn Section of Blink is a fun way for our users to earn sats by  learning the basics about bitcoin.&#x20;

Go to the Earn section of your Blink (bottom right). Answer the questions correctly to earn some sats that will appear automatically on your wallet.\
\
You can only earn the sats after you complete the quiz once. Nevertheless, you can always comeback for a refresh!
