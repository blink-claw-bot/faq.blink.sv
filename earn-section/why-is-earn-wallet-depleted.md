# Why is Earn wallet depleted?

The Earn wallet may occasionally be depleted as we regularly add funds to ensure a sustainable earning experience for our users. If you find that your Earn wallet is currently empty, we recommend trying again in the upcoming days. Earn wallet will be replenished with new funds in 24 hours to continue supporting your earning activities. Thank you for your patience and understanding. If you have any further questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.
