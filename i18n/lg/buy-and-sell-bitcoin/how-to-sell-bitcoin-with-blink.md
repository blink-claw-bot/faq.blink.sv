# Nsobola ntya okutunda bitcoin ne Blink?

1. Ggulawo app ya Blink onyige ku kabonero akalaga graph y’emiwendo waggulu ku kkono

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot01.jpg" width="300">

2. Koona ku `Gula ate otunde bitcoin` wansi ku lutimbe

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot02.jpg" width="300">

3. Londa ensi akawunti yo mu bbanka gy’eri era onyige ku `Tunda Bitcoin`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot03.jpg" width="300">

5. Yingiza ssente z'ogenda okutunda mu USD, jjuzaamu ebikwata ku bbanka yo w'ogenda okufuna ssente n'onyiga ku `Ekiddirira`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot07.jpg" width="300">

6. Jjuza ebikukwatako nga bikwatagana n'ebikwata ku akawunti yo mu bbanka, ssaamu email gy'osobola okutuukirira n'onyiga ku `Ekiddirira`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot08.jpg" width="300">

8. Kakasa ebikwata ku ndagiriro ebirimu amannya go ne nnamba ya akawunti ya bbanka (ebikyusiddwa wano) era onyige ku `Kakasa`

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot09.jpg" width="300">

9.  Londa oba oyagala okusasula okuva ku bbalansi yo eya BTC oba USD (Stablesats) era onyige `Ekiddirira` okusasula

    <img src="/.gitbook/assets/blink_fiat_app_screenshot10.jpg" width="300">

10. Ekiragiro kijja kukolebwako mu ngeri ya otomatiki, naye kiyinza okutwala wakati w’essaawa 1 n’olunaku 1 ssente okutuuka ku akawunti yo mu bbanka. Nkusaba olondoole endagiriro yo eya email okulaba ebipya era otuukirire [support@blink.sv](mailto:support@blink.sv) bw’oba ​​olina ekikweraliikiriza.
