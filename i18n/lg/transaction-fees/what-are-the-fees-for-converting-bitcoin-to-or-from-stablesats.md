# Bisale ki ebisasulwa okukyusa Bitcoin okudda oba okuva mu Stablesats?

Stablesats kintu kya njawulo ekiweebwa Blink ekisobozesa abakozesa okukyusa Bitcoin okudda ku akawunti eyenkanankana ne USD mu waleti. Kino kiyamba nnyo eri abakozesa abayinza okweraliikirira okukyukakyuka okw’ekiseera ekitono okwa Bitcoin. Stablesats zikuumibwa nga tezikyukakyuka mu muwendo nga pulojekiti ya buli omu ekozesa enkola y'okusuubula ebivaamu okukola "eby'obutonde ebya USD".

Bw’oba ​​okyusa okuva ku Bitcoin Wallet yo okudda mu waleti yo eya USD, okusaasaana kwa **0.20%** kusasulwa era ne kukuumibwa Blink. Okusaasaana kuno kukwata ku ssente z’okukyusa Bitcoin okudda mu Stablesats n’okukakasa nti omuwendo ogwenkanankana ne USD gukuumibwa mu waleti yo.

Bw’oba ​​okyusa okuva ku USD Wallet yo okudda mu Bitcoin Wallet yo, okusaasaana kwa **0.20%** nakwo kusasulwa era ne kukuumibwa Blink. Ssente zino zisasula ssente z’okukyusa Stablesats okudda mu Bitcoin, wamu ne ssente zonna ez’okutunda ezikwatagana n’okukyusa.

Tukkiriza nti ekintu kya Stablesats kiwa omugaso omukulu eri abakozesa baffe naddala abo abapya mu Bitcoin oba abayinza okweraliikirira okukyukakyuka.&#x20;
