# Nsobola wa okukakasa embeera y’okutunda ku on-chain?

Osobola okukakasa embeera y’okutunda ku lujegere ng’okozesa omunoonyereza wa blockchain nga [mempool.com](https://mempool.com). Blockchain explorer kye kimu ku bikozesebwa ebikusobozesa okunoonya n’okulaba emirimu gyonna egyabaddewo ku Bitcoin blockchain.

Okukebera embeera y’okutunda kwo, ojja kwetaaga ID y’okutunda oba hash. Ebiseera ebisinga bisobola okusangibwa mu byafaayo by’okutunda mu waleti yo oba ku lupapula olukakasa okutunda.

Bw’omala okufuna ID y’okutunda oba hash, osobola okugiyingiza mu bbaala y’okunoonya ey'okulambuula blockchain, era ejja kukulaga ebikwata ku nkolagana, omuli omuwendo gw’ebikakasiddwa n’embeera eriwo kati.
