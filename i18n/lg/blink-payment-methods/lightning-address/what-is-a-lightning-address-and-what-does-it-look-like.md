# Lightning Address kye ki, era kifaanana ki?

Lightning Addresses kika kya kusaba kwa Lightning Network okusasula nga kwesigamiziddwa ku nkola ya LNURL esobozesa abakozesa okufuna okusasula kwa bitcoin butereevu mu waleti yaabwe eya Lightning. Ziringa endagiriro ya email, naye mu kifo kya email, osobola okufuna ssente za Lightning nazo.

Lightning Addresses zisobola okulabika mu ngeri ey’enjawulo okusinziira ku mpeereza oyo afuna ssente z’akozesa.&#x20;

Okugeza, Endagiriro ya Lightning y’omukozesa wa Blink ejja kufaanana username@blink.sv, ate Endagiriro ya Lightning y’omukozesa wa Wallet of Satoshi eyinza okufaanana username@walletofsatoshi.com.&#x20;

Ka kibeere nti nkola ki, Endagiriro za Lightning zonna za njawulo eri oyo azifuna, ekibasobozesa okufuna ssente za Lightning mu ngeri ey’obukuumi era mu ngeri ennyangu.
