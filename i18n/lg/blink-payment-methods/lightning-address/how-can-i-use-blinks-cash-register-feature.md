# Nkozesa ntya Point of Sale (PoS oba Cash Register) mu Blink?

Waliwo engeri bbiri ez'okukozesa blink Point of Sale:

**Eky'okulondako 1:**

1. Nyiga ku menu waggulu ku ddyo ku ssimu yo eya Blink
2. Nyiga ku "Ngeri z'okusasulwa"
3. Wano oja kusanga ennyunga yo eya v
4. Osobola okugabana enkolagana eno n’abakozi bo, bakasitoma bo, oba okugigattako butereevu ku mikutu gyo egy’empuliziganya, omukutu gwo, oba blog okufuna ssente.

**Eky'okulondako 2:**

1. Genda ku [pay.blink.sv](https://pay.blink.sv/)
2. Yingiza erinnya lyo ery'omukozesa wa Blink oba Yingira ne Blink
3. Tandika okufuna ssente ng'okozesa blink Point of Sale yo ey'enjawulo

Bw’okozesa Ekitundu ky'okutunda, osobola bulungi okufuna ssente butereevu ku akawunti zombi (BTC / USD) nga tewesigamye ku kyuma kimu. Kye kigonjoola ekirungi eri bannannyini bizinensi entonotono abeetaaga enkola ya POS ekola nga tebalina ssente ezongerwako ez’enkola z’okusasula ez’ennono.
