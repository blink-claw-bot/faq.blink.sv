# Nkola ntya akawunti ne Blink?

Okukola akawunti ya Blink nkola nnyangu erimu emitendera gino wammanga:

1. Wano wefunire enkola ya Blink okuva mu app store y’essimu yo oba [kozesa apk ku Android](/creating-a-blink-account/how-to-install-the-apk-from-github.md)
2. Londa `Ensengeka mu nsonda waggulu ku ddyo ku lutimbe olusokerwaako
3. Londa bendera okusinziira ku nnamba y'essimu yo
4. Yingiza ennamba y’essimu yo onyige ku ddyo okumaliriza puzzle
5. Yingiza koodi esindikiddwa nga obubaka obwa SMS

## Yongera endagiriro ya email (sikya teeka)
Okwongerako n’endagiriro ya email kikusobozesa okugikozesa okuyingira kale teweetaaga kwesigama ku nnamba ya ssimu. Emitendera gy'olina okwongerako n'endagiriro ya email:

1. londa menu waggulu ku ddyo era onyige ku `Akawunti`
2. londa `Email (okuyingira)`
3. ssaamu endagiriro ya email era omalirize okukakasa okusobozesa okuyingira ne email

## Kola errinnya ly'ogenda okukozesa (sikya teeka)
Erinnya ly'omukozesa lye kimanyisa okumyansa kwo okw'omunda era era kikola nga [endagiriro ya Lightning](/blink-payment-methods/README.md)
6. Londa `Ensengeka`
7. Londa `Endagiriro yo eya Blink` era onyige mu `Kola Endagiriro yo eya Blink`
8. Londa erinnya ly'omukozesa ku akawunti yo. Erinnya lyo ery'omukozesa teririna kuba na bubonero oba bifo bya njawulo. Singa erinnya ly’omukozesa ly’oyagala teririiwo, osobola okwongerako ennamba oba ennukuta endala okukebera oba liriwo.
9. Nyiga ku `Kola Endagiriro yo eya Blink` okumaliriza okukola akawunti yo
