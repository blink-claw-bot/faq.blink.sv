# Nkola ntya wesifuna SMS?

Bw'oba tofunye bubaka bwa SMS okumaliriza okwewandiisa kwa Blink, kakasa nti essimu yo erina olukusa lw'okuweereza obubaka ku ssimu ez'ensi yonna eza SMS oba okukwatagana ne yintaneeti okufuna obukodyo okukakasibwa ku WhatsApp.

Bw’oba n’okutuusa kati tonnafuna koodi y’okukakasa oluvannyuma lw’okugezaako emirundi esatu, tuukirira abawagira Blink okufuna obuyambi obutereevu.
