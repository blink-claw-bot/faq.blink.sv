# Okulinyisa omutindo gwa akawunti ey'okugezesa okutuuka ku mutendera 1

Okuddukanya Blink ku masimu agamu (okuddukanya OS etakyusiddwa, ya sitokisi) kisobozesa okuggulawo akawunti ya Kugezesa nga tokozesezza wadde ennamba y’essimu.

Akawunti y'okugezesa erina akakwakkulizo ka ssente ntono era tebakkirizibwa kusindika oba okufuna on-chain.

Okulinyisa omutindo okudda ku mutendera 1 kijja n'emiganyulo:

* &#x20;okwongera ku mitendera gy'okutunda
* &#x20;obusobozi okuzzaawo akawunti ng’okozesa ennamba y’essimu ewandiisiddwa ng’okozesa SMS / WhatsApp oba email
* okusobozesa emirimu nga okusindika n’okufuna ku lujegere, okuteekawo erinnya ly’omukozesa, endagiriro y’omulabe, omukutu gwa Point of Sale (PoS oba Cash Register), paycode etakyuuka n’ebirala.


Okulinnya okuva ku akawunti y'oKugezesal (ddaala 0) okudda ku ddaala 1 ojja kwetaaga okukakasa ennamba y’essimu ng’okozesa SMS oba WhatsApp:

1. Mu walleti ya Blink ey'okugezesa londa menu waggulu ku ddyo era onyige ku `Akawunti`
2. londa `Okutereka/okulongoosa akawunti`
3. maliriza okukola ku akawunti eyateekebwawo n’ennamba y’essimu


Tukukubiriza okussaako endagiriro ya email nayo osobole okuddamu okukola ne email era teweetaaga kwesigama ku nnamba ya ssimu:

Okwongerako n’okuyingira ku email (efunibwa singa ennamba y’essimu yayongerwako emabegako):

1. londa menu waggulu ku ddyo era onyige ku `Akawunti`
2. londa `Email (okuyingira)`
3. ssaamu endagiriro ya email era omalirize okukakasa okusobozesa okuyingira ne email
