# Nnyinza ntya okwongera balance ku account yange eya Blink?

Waliwo engeri eziwerako ez'okwongera ssente ku akawunti yo eya Blink:

1. Funa oba funa bitcoin: tunda ebintu ebimu oba osasulwa mu bitcoin mu waleti yo eya Blink.
2. Gula bitcoin ng’oyita mu Blink: eri abakozesa mu El Salvador [balongoosezza akawunti yaabwe okutuuka ku ddaala 2](https://faq.blink.sv/using-blink/how-can-i-change-my-transaction-limits-on-my-blink-account) okugula n'okutunda bitcoin kisangibwa ne Blink:
    - Mu app ya Blink laba: [Ngula ntya Bitcoin ne Blink?](https://faq.blink.sv/buy-and-sell-bitcoin/how-to-buy-bitcoin-with-blink)
    - Ekirala osobola okukozesa omukutu: [fiat.blink.sv](https://fiat.blink.sv)
3. Weereza bitcoin okuva mu kuwaanyisa oba waleti endala ng’okozesa Lightning Network.
4. Weereza bitcoin okuva mu kuwaanyisa oba waleti endala ku lujegere ku ndagiriro ya waleti ya Bitcoin eweereddwa.
5. Gula bitcoin okuva ku banu abalala oba okuyita mu ATM.

Tukusaba omanye nti ebisale biyinza okusasulwa okusinziira ku ngeri gy’olonze okwongera ssente ku akawunti yo eya Blink.
