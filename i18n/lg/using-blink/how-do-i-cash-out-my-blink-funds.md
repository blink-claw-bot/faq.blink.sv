# Nzijayo ntya ssente ku Blink?

Okuggyayo ssente zo eza Blink, osobola okukozesa enkola zino wammanga:

1. Ku bakozesa mu El Salvador [balongoosezza akawunti yaabwe okutuuka ku ddaala 2](https://faq.blink.sv/using-blink/how-can-i-change-my-transaction-limits-on-my-blink-account) okutunda n'okugula bitcoin esangibwa ne Blink:
    - Mu app ya Blink laba: [Nsobola ntya okutunda bitcoin ne Blink?](https://faq.blink.sv/buy-and-sell-bitcoin/how-to-sell-bitcoin-with-blink)
    - Ekirala osobola okukozesa omukutu guno: [fiat.blink.sv](https://fiat.blink.sv)
2. Tunda bitcoin peer to peer okugiwanyisiganya n'ensimbi ya fiat.
3. Funa ATM ya Bitcoin okumpi naawe ng’okozesa omukutu nga [Coin Atm Radar](https://coinatmradar.com/).
4. Tunda Bitcoin yo ng’oyita mu kifo eky’okuwanyisiganya ssente nga Bitfinex, Kraken, oba ebirala ebiwagira Blink.&#x20;

Tukusaba omanye nti ebisale biyinza okusasulwa okusinziira ku nkola y’okuwanyisiganya gy’okozesa era ng’enkola zino ziyinza okwawukana. Bulijjo kirungi okunoonyereza n’okugeraageranya ebisale n’emiwendo gy’abagaba obujjanjabi ab’enjawulo nga tonnalonda nkola ya kuggyamu ssente zo.
