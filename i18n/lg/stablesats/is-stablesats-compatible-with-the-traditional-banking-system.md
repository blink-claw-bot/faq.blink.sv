# Stablesats ekwatagana n'enkola ya bbanka ey'edda?

N’okutuusa kati, Stablesats ekwatagana n’omukutu gw’okusasula ogwa Bitcoin gwokka era tekwatagana butereevu na nkola za bbanka ez’ennono. Kino kiri bwe kityo kubanga Stablesats ekoleddwa okukola munda mu Lightning Network, enkola ya layer-2 ezimbiddwa waggulu ku Bitcoin blockchain. N’ekyavaamu, abakozesa Stablesats basobola okunyumirwa emigaso gy’okukuuma bbalansi ezenkanankana ne ddoola mu waleti yaabwe eya Lightning nga tebeesigamye ku stablecoins oba okugatta awamu fiat.

Wadde nga Stablesats eyinza obutakwatagana butereevu na nkola za bbanka ez’ennono, eraga omutendera gw’okugatta Bitcoin yennyini. Mu kiseera kino, okuwanyisiganya ddoola za Stablesats ku Doola mu nkola ya bbanka ey’ennono, kyetaagisa okuyita mu mpeereza y’okuwanyisiganya ya BTC/USD. Wadde nga kino kiri bwe kityo, enkola ya Stablesats ey’okutandikawo okukendeeza ku kukyukakyuka kwa Bitcoin okw’ekiseera ekitono n’okukuuma amaanyi g’okugula ga sats esikiriza bangi ku bakozesa Lightning Network. Nga balina Stablesats, abakozesa basobola okukwata, okusindika, n’okufuna ddoola mu waleti yaabwe eya Lightning, nga beetoloola obwetaavu bw’omuntu ow’okusatu agaba stablecoin oba okukwatagana obutereevu n’enkola ya bbanka ey’ennono.
