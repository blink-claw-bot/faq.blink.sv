# Blink Merchant Map

Welcome to the Merchant Map section of the Blink FAQ. In this section, we will cover common questions related to the Merchant Map feature available in the Blink app. The Merchant Map allows you to easily find merchants in your area that accept Bitcoin as a payment method, making it easier for you to use your Bitcoin in day-to-day transactions. Whether you're looking for a coffee shop or a clothing store, the Merchant Map has got you covered. Let's dive into some frequently asked questions about this feature.
