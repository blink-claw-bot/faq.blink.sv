# How can I add my business to Blink's Merchant Map?

If you would like to add your business to the Blink map, please follow these simple steps:

1. Create a Blink wallet using a username for your business.
2. Contact the Blink support team and provide a screenshot of your username, as well as your business name and GPS coordinates (or share your location through WhatsApp).
3. Once you have provided all the necessary information, the Blink support team will confirm your business listing on the map.

By adding your business to the BBW map, you will make it easier for potential customers to find you and pay with Bitcoin using the Blink app. Don't hesitate to contact our support team if you have any questions or need assistance with the process.
