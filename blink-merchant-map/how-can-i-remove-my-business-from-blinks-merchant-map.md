# How can I remove my business from Blink's merchant map?

\
If for any reason you would like to remove your business from the Blink Merchant Map, simply contact our support team and we will be happy to assist you with the process. You can reach out to us via email, phone, or live chat, and our dedicated support staff will promptly respond to your request.

We understand that business needs and circumstances can change, and we want to make sure that our map feature accurately reflects the current state of all registered businesses. Our team will work with you to ensure that your business is removed from the map as quickly and smoothly as possible, while also addressing any questions or concerns you may have.

Please note that once your business is removed from the map, it may take some time for the changes to fully propagate through our system. However, rest assured that we will do everything we can to ensure that your business is no longer listed on the Blink Merchant map.
